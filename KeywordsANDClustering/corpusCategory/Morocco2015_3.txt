Recent policy action and a more favorable external environment have yielded macroeconomic improvements, but the reform momentum needs to be maintained in order to secure longer term stability and raise growth potential (Box 1). Despite positive developments in certain sectors (industry, energy, and telecommunication), there are still mixed signals about non-agricultural growth and risks remain, in particular regarding future external demand. Securing stronger buffers and raising potential growth while significantly reducing unemployment and inequalities will require: Maintaining an adequate policy stance; Securing macro-financial sustainability; Reinforcing monetary transmission; Establishing a more growth-friendly and inclusive environment.
