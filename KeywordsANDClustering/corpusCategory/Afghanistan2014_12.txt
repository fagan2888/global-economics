The authorities intend to enact FATF-consistent AML/CFT legislation and adopt a national strategy that will include money laundering and terrorist financing risk assessments; measures to investigate and confiscate the proceeds of crime; measures to enhance the transparency of legal persons and non-profit organizations; and implementation of a risk-based AML/CFT supervisory Page 27 regime. The authorities also plan to implement a regime for the declaration of cross-border transportation of currency, negotiable bearer instruments, and gold with the necessary enforcement mechanisms.
