REDUCING VULNERABILITIES Despite the broadly favorable outlook, Malta faces important challenges: public debt is still high; non-performing loans are elevated; the cost of capital is relatively high despite abundant liquidity; and maintaining competitiveness is increasingly difficult. To raise growth in a sustainable manner and reduce vulnerabilities, the policy agenda should focus on: 1) strengthening fiscal sustainability; 2) maintaining financial stability; and 3) enhancing competitiveness and reducing the cost of capital.
