The authorities continue to strengthen the statistical system . In addition to the several improvements listed above, INE is taking steps to improve the outdated consumer price index (CPI). The household survey completed in 2011 is providing the basis for reweighting the CPI components and introducing new goods and services to its basket (both were updated last about twenty years ago). The introduction of the new CPI by end-December 2013 (structural benchmark) is expected to greatly improve the measurement of inflation (Informational Annex).
