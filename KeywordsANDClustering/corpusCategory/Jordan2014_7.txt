The reviews focused on program implementation and laid the groundwork for medium-term consolidation. The second review adjusted the fiscal program to help cushion disruptions in gas supplies and the impact of the Syria conflict. With reserves rebuilt and a sound energy sector strategy in place, the third and fourth reviews centered on measures to underpin the planned consolidation and further reduce program risks (Box 8). Staff stressed that strong ownership is critical for the success of the program and offered TA, which could help reduce implementation risks.
