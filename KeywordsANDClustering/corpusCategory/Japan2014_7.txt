The current account surplus declined further in 2013 to 0.7 percent of GDP. Although the yen depreciated by about 23¾ percent between October 2012–April 2014 (reversing a 27¾ percent real appreciation during July 2007–September 2012), so far this has failed to arrest the decline in Japan’s trade balance.
