ADJUSTMENT, AND REFORM Discussions focused on the challenges of addressing vulnerabilities, managing growth, and implementing reforms. Vulnerabilities have reached the point that addressing them is an urgent priority. Adjustment cannot be too sharp, as this would be destabilizing, but at the same time it cannot be delayed, as that would only make the problem bigger. Reducing vulnerabilities will inevitably lead to slower growth in the near term. Over the medium term, potential growth will be determined by the progress with structural reforms.
